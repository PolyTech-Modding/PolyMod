Welcome to the PolyTech Mod Index.


PolyTech is the brainchild of Conqu3red and Arglin Kampling. We founded PolyTech in June of 2020 with the initial intention of bringing technical marvels from real life into Poly Bridge 2. In the following month, however, the PolyTech Modding community was born, and for months the community had grown in privacy.

Now, we're happy to bring modding to the public! Welcome to PolyTech.
